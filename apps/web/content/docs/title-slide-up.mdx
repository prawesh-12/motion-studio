## When to use

The flagship intro slate. Each word in the headline rises out of an invisible baseline using a classic masked-text reveal — smooth decelerating easing, tight letter spacing, generous size. The optional subtitle fades in once the headline lands.

Reach for this when the title itself is the moment — product launches, chapter cards, opening slates. Keep the headline short — one to four words reads cleanest at 1080p. Pair a near-black `textColor` with a near-white `backgroundColor` (or invert) for the cleanest contrast; the subtitle auto-tones based on the text color.
