## Usage

The popup slides down from the top of the frame, holds, and slides back out. It mimics the iOS lock-screen notification banner — useful for fake-text-message reels, product reveals, or any moment that benefits from the "you've got mail" instinct.

Keep `body` short — one or two short lines reads cleanest at video resolution.
