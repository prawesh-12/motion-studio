## When to use

A character-by-character typewriter reveal in a monospaced face, with a caret that blinks once the headline lands. Reads as developer-y, terminal-coded, "live demo" — perfect for product hunts, dev tool intros, or any moment where the headline benefits from feeling actively typed.

Headline length matters more here than in slide-up — every character costs frames. For a 12-character headline at the default speed (2 frames/char) the headline finishes around frame ~32. If your composition duration is short, shorten the headline rather than racing the typing.
