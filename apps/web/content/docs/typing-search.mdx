## Usage

A short demo slate for product walkthroughs, landing-page hero loops, or "how does this thing get found" explainers. The bar springs in, the query types out with a blinking caret, then a mouse cursor enters from off-screen, glides to the search button, and presses it — accent-colored pulse ring confirms the click.

Keep the `query` short and concrete (≤ 30 characters reads cleanly at 720p). `accentColor` controls the search button and the click pulse; pick a brand-aligned color.
